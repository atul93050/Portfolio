Ingredients for Paneer Butter Masala

  • Paneer (cottage cheese) - 200 grams
  • Butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Onion - 1 large, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 large, pureed
  • Ginger-Garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Green chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Cashew nuts - 10-12, soaked and blended into a paste
  • Heavy cream - 1/4 cup
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  • Water - as needed

Instructions:

Prepare the Base:

Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a pan over medium heat.Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they become golden brown.Add the ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.

Add Spices:

Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few more minutes.

Add Cashew Paste:

Add the blended cashew paste to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes. This adds a creamy texture to the gravy.

Cook Paneer:

Add cubed paneer to the pan and mix gently to coat the paneer with the masala.Pour in water as needed to achieve the desired consistency of the gravy.Simmer for 5-7 minutes.

Finish with Cream:

Stir in the heavy cream and garam masala. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Garnish and Serve:

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.

Enjoy your delicious Paneer Butter Masala!
