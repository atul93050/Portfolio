Lucknow

Lucknow, the capital city of Uttar Pradesh in India, is a vibrant city known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and architectural grandeur. It has been a prominent cultural and political center for centuries and is often referred to as the "City of Nawabs" due to its deep-rooted connection with the Nawabs of Awadh.
